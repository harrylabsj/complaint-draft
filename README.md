# Complaint Draft

## Use Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Unpaid Loan Recovery
A lender lent ¥200,000 to a friend with a written IOU, but the borrower has refused to repay for over a year despite repeated demands. **What the skill does:** Gathers the loan facts, identifies the breach of contract claim, calculates damages including principal and accrued interest, and generates a structured civil complaint framework with all required sections (parties, factual allegations, cause of action, prayer for relief). **Expected outcome:** A complete complaint draft ready for attorney review, with properly formatted claims for debt recovery under Chinese civil law.

### Scenario 2: Property Purchase Dispute
A buyer signed a purchase agreement and paid a ¥500,000 deposit for an apartment, but the seller now refuses to transfer title and claims the contract is void. **What the skill does:** Organizes the transaction timeline, identifies claims for specific performance or contract rescission with damages, and structures the complaint framework with appropriate jurisdictional and venue statements. **Expected outcome:** A well-organized complaint that clearly articulates the buyer's legal position and requested remedies, serving as a solid foundation for court filing.

### Scenario 3: Service Contract Breach
A company paid a marketing agency ¥100,000 for a 6-month campaign, but the agency delivered only one month of work and ceased communication. **What the skill does:** Structures the breach of contract claim by organizing the service agreement terms, documenting the agency's non-performance, and calculating damages including refund of prepaid fees and consequential losses. **Expected outcome:** A professional complaint framework with detailed factual allegations that clearly establishes the elements of breach of contract.

## Overview

This skill helps users generate civil complaint document frameworks based on case facts. It structures the complaint according to standard legal document formats, organizing facts, claims, and relief sought.

**⚠️ Important Disclaimer**: This tool provides document framework assistance only. It does not constitute legal advice, nor does it guarantee any particular legal outcome. Always consult a qualified attorney before filing any legal action.

## When to Use This Skill

- Preparing to file a civil lawsuit
- Organizing case facts into legal format
- Understanding what information is needed for a complaint
- Getting a framework to discuss with an attorney
